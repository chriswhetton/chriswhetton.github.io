Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self-awareness? Mindfulness is a path to self-awareness; through paying attention to what is happening in the present moment, taking both positive and negative experiences and associated feelings we build self-awareness.

Try at least one of the self-awareness practices. What was your experience, how did you feel? I practised body scan in a seated position. Although feeling relaxed, the body felt heavy in the areas of focus with neutral emotions.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section? Everything in moderation. It's good to be self-aware and have self-belief, including recognising one's strengths and weaknesses, but over analysing can lead to paralysis.
