Search Inside Yourself - Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it? I tried something similar to this late last year at work due to misunderstandings around work to be carried out. I had an individual 'loop' back their understanding of the conversation. Unfortunately, even though they understood, they still went off on a different tangent. Without knowing, I performed 'dipping' as I organised a meeting with other participants to establish whether it was my communication style that was causing misunderstandings.

A caveat, this needs to be done in such a way that it's not condescending and treating colleagues like children.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice? I've found walking in the past works wonders, even if its just a quick walk around the block. I will focus more on the breathing aspect and awareness of my body.
